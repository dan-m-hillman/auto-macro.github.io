Why is "Restore premium" button not restoring premium for me?

If you've previously bought premium with your apple account, you should be able to restore Auto Soundboard back to premium using the same Apple account. A few users have been experiencing issues with restoring premium.

I'm not sure what is happening with the Apple Purchase SDK for restoring purchases. HOWEVER there is a workaround.

If you click on the "Purchase premium" button again, this will handle unlocking premium for you. Essentially since you've already bought premium, Apple will NOT charge you again for something that you already own. So the "Purchase premium" should do the same thing as what the "Restore premium" button should be doing. Give that a try. If it's still not working, delete the Auto Soundboard app, redownload it from the store, and try the "Purchase premium" button again. That seems to work for other users who have reached out to me with similar issues.

As I have explained above, Apple won't charge you again if you've already purchased this in-app purchase product with your apple id previously. Thanks again for purchasing Auto Soundboard and apologies for the issues you have experienced.
